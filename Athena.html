Welcome to Athena: FTC Team 9657

We are a group of 19 girls from Evergreen Valley High School's Robotics Club committed to building, coding, and eating Oreos! As a rookie team participating in this year's FTC RES-Q mission, we are facing new challenges and experiences as expected.
So what is RES-Q? FIRST asked FTC around the world to design a robot that will be able to do some of the following:
- Pick up debris to place in the floor or mountain goals
- Trigger a color beacon based on alliance color
- Climb up a mountain and hang
